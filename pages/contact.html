Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by contacting us through this form, calling our office, or visiting our campus. Our enrollment team will guide you through the process and help you choose the right program.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most of our programs. We also accept various funding options including employer sponsorship and financial aid programs.

Are courses available online?

Many of our courses are available in online, in-person, or hybrid formats. We offer flexible learning options to accommodate different schedules and learning preferences.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, upon successful completion of our programs, you'll receive industry-recognized certificates that can enhance your professional credentials and career prospects.

Do you offer career support?

Absolutely! Our career services team provides job placement assistance, resume review, interview preparation, and ongoing career guidance to help you succeed.

What are the class sizes?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. Most classes have 15-25 students to maximize interaction and support.
